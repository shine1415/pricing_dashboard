# A/B Pricing Test Report: Male iOS Users - Rich Region

**Test Period:** January 12 - March 19, 2026, with January 30 removed (66 analyzed days)  
**Platform:** iOS  
**Region:** Rich (Germany, Austria, Switzerland, US, UK, Nordics)  
**Gender:** Male  
**Sample Size:** 7,840 users (Group A: 3,928 | Group B: 3,912)  
**Data Quality:** Correct P-values only. Jan 30 removed as a mixed pricing-transition day. March 20-24 excluded due to Ramadan promo contamination.

---

## Executive Summary

**Winner: Group B**

- Group B achieved **21.96% CVR** vs Group A's **19.63%** (+11.9% lift)
- Group B generated **€1.67/user** vs Group A's **€1.51/user** (+10.6%)
- Group B also led on modeled revenue: **€6,529.55** vs **€5,919.49**

**Recommendation:** Deploy **P18-P40** for iOS Rich. Unlike Poor Region, the lower Period 2 ladder improves both conversion and daily revenue efficiency.

---

## Overall Performance

### Group A
- Exposures: **3,928** unique users
- Converters: **771** unique users
- CVR: **19.63%**
- Revenue per user: **€1.51**

### Group B
- Exposures: **3,912** unique users
- Converters: **859** unique users
- CVR: **21.96%**
- Revenue per user: **€1.67**

**Lift:** +11.9% CVR and +10.6% revenue per user in favor of Group B.

---

## Period Comparison

**Period definitions:**
- Period 1: **Jan 12-29** (18 days)
- Jan 30: removed
- Period 2: **Jan 31-Mar 19** (48 days)

### Group A: maintained higher ladder
| Metric | Period 1 | Period 2 | Change |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| CVR | 11.40% | 16.84% | **+48%** |
| Rev/User | €0.64 | €1.27 | +99% |
| Rev/User/Day | €0.0335 | €0.0264 | **-25%** |

### Group B: lowered to P18-P40 in Period 2
| Metric | Period 1 | Period 2 | Change |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| CVR | 11.56% | 19.82% | **+71%** |
| Rev/User | €0.45 | €1.62 | +260% |
| Rev/User/Day | €0.0238 | €0.0337 | **+39%** |

**Read:** Group B is the clear Rich Region winner. The Period 2 ladder drove stronger conversion and higher daily revenue efficiency, which is the cleanest win-win pattern in the package.

---

## Recommendation

**Deploy P18-P40 for iOS Rich male users.**

Why:
- It wins overall CVR and revenue per user.
- It is stronger than the maintained higher ladder after the Jan 30 exclusion.
- Period 2 performance suggests Rich Region still has room for lower pricing without damaging monetization.

**Bottom line:** iOS Rich is meaningfully more price-elastic than iOS Poor, and **P18-P40 is the better production ladder**.
