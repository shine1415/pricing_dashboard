# A/B Pricing Test Report: Male Android Users - Rich Region

**Test Period:** January 12 - March 19, 2026, with January 30 removed (66 analyzed days)  
**Platform:** Android  
**Region:** Rich (Germany, Austria, Switzerland, US, UK, Nordics)  
**Gender:** Male  
**Sample Size:** 1,897 users (Group A: 902 | Group B: 995)  
**Data Quality:** Correct P-values only. Jan 30 removed as a mixed pricing-transition day. March 20-24 excluded due to Ramadan promo contamination.

---

## Executive Summary

**Winner: Group B on CVR; mixed on revenue per user**

- Group B achieved **19.20% CVR** vs Group A's **16.30%** (+17.8% lift)
- Group A generated **€0.85/user** vs Group B's **€0.73/user**
- Group B delivered more converters: **191** vs **147**

**Recommendation:** Keep **P18-P40** as the leading candidate for Android Rich if conversion growth matters most, but note that this segment is less clean than iOS Rich because revenue per user still favors Group A.

---

## Overall Performance

### Group A
- Exposures: **902** unique users
- Converters: **147** unique users
- CVR: **16.30%**
- Revenue per user: **€0.85**

### Group B
- Exposures: **995** unique users
- Converters: **191** unique users
- CVR: **19.20%**
- Revenue per user: **€0.73**

**Read:** Android Rich is the least straightforward segment. Lower prices clearly improve conversion, but the monetization story is closer to flat-to-negative.

---

## Period Comparison

**Period definitions:**
- Period 1: **Jan 12-29** (18 days)
- Jan 30: removed
- Period 2: **Jan 31-Mar 19** (48 days)

### Group A: maintained higher ladder
| Metric | Period 1 | Period 2 | Change |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| CVR | 8.16% | 13.92% | **+71%** |
| Rev/User | €0.44 | €0.64 | +45% |
| Rev/User/Day | €0.0230 | €0.0133 | **-43%** |

### Group B: lowered to P18-P40 in Period 2
| Metric | Period 1 | Period 2 | Change |
|--------|----------|----------|--------|
| CVR | 7.48% | 18.07% | **+142%** |
| Rev/User | €0.27 | €0.63 | +133% |
| Rev/User/Day | €0.0141 | €0.0132 | **-10%** |

**Read:** Group B handled Period 2 better than Group A on both conversion growth and daily revenue resilience, but this is still not as clean a revenue-positive result as iOS Rich.

---

## Recommendation

**Primary choice: P18-P40 if conversion and volume are the priority.**

Caveat:
- If pure revenue per user is the top KPI, this segment still deserves caution because Group A remains higher on that metric overall.

**Bottom line:** Android Rich supports lower pricing better than the old high ladder, but it should be framed as a conversion-led recommendation rather than an unambiguous revenue win.
